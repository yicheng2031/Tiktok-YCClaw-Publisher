# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-04-22  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-22  

This Privacy Policy explains how **YCClaw Publisher** (the “Service”) operated by **YC Studio** (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Service.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- Account information (e.g., email, name, organization name) if you create an OpenClaw account.
- Support requests and communications.

### 1.2 Content You Upload or Provide
- Media files you select for publishing (e.g., photos/videos) and related metadata you provide (titles, captions, descriptions).

### 1.3 Information from Third-Party Platforms (e.g., TikTok)
If you connect a third-party account (such as TikTok), we may receive:
- Identifiers such as **open_id** (or similar account identifiers).
- OAuth tokens (access token and refresh token) and their expiry metadata.
- Posting status information returned by the platform APIs (e.g., publish_id, processing status, error codes).

### 1.4 Technical and Usage Data
- Device/browser information, IP address, timestamps, logs, and analytics events.
- Cookies or similar technologies (for web experiences), if applicable.

## 2. How We Use Information
We use information to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain the Service.
- Authenticate you and manage your connected third-party accounts.
- Upload content to third-party platforms at your direction and provide status tracking.
- Improve and secure the Service, prevent fraud/abuse, and debug issues.
- Communicate with you about updates, support, and administrative messages.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

---

## 3. How We Share Information
We may share information in the following ways:

### 3.1 With Third-Party Platforms You Connect
When you instruct us to publish content, we transmit your content and required metadata to the connected platform (e.g., TikTok) using the permissions you granted.

### 3.2 Service Providers
We may share information with vendors who help us operate the Service (e.g., hosting, storage/CDN, analytics, error monitoring), under contractual confidentiality and security obligations.

### 3.3 Legal and Safety
We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security of OpenClaw, users, or the public.

### 3.4 Business Transfers
If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

---

## 4. Data Retention
We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

**Posting media retention (recommended statement):**  
Uploaded media files used for publishing may be stored temporarily to complete publishing and status tracking, then deleted after a reasonable period (e.g., 7–30 days), unless you choose to keep them for reuse or longer retention is required by law.

## 5. Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Choices and Rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
- Object to or restrict certain processing.
- Withdraw consent (where applicable).

### 6.1 Disconnecting TikTok (or Other Platforms)
You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time through the Service. Disconnecting revokes our ability to act on your behalf (subject to platform behavior and token revocation).
